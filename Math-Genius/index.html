Privacy Policy

MathGenius PRO • Official Protocol

Introduction

Welcome to MathGenius PRO. Your privacy is not just a feature; it is our fundamental principle. This application is developed by Abu Bakor under Hanbak Private Limited. We have designed this experience to be completely private, ensuring that your cognitive training remains your business alone.

Zero-Data Collection

MathGenius PRO does not collect any personal data. We do not require account creation, email addresses, phone numbers, or social media linking. No user identifiers, device IDs, or IP addresses are ever stored on our servers because we do not use servers for user data.

Local Storage

All game progress, including High Scores, Level Progression, and Daily Life rewards, is stored locally on your device. This data remains on your physical hardware and is only accessible by the application on that specific device. If you clear your browser data or uninstall the app, this local progress will be permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising networks. MathGenius PRO is a clean, distraction-free environment that respects your digital boundaries.
